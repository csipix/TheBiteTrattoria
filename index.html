The Bite Trattoria

Welcome to The Bite Trattoria, where every dish tells a story and every sip invites you into a world of flavor. Nestled in the heart of Granada, our restaurant is a haven for those seeking culinary delights in a cozy and inviting atmosphere.

At The Bite Trattoria, we blend the warmth of a traditional trattoria with the vibrant energy of a contemporary bistro. Our menu is a celebration of Italian-inspired cuisine, crafted with care and passion by our talented chefs. From classic pasta dishes bursting with homemade sauces to sizzling pizzas straight from our wood-fired oven, each bite is a journey through the rich culinary landscape of Italy.
